Senior Strategy Consultant

Full-time · Johannesburg · Competitive Salary

About the Role

Lead strategic advisory engagements with start-ups, SMEs and corporate clients. Drive growth planning, market analysis and transformation initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead client engagements from discovery to delivery
  • Develop strategic roadmaps and business plans
  • Conduct market research and competitive analysis
  • Present insights and recommendations to C-suite executives
  • Mentor junior consultants and analysts

Requirements

  • 5+ years in strategy consulting or corporate strategy
  • MBA or relevant postgraduate qualification
  • Proven track record in business transformation
  • Excellent presentation and stakeholder management skills
  • Experience in African markets preferred
