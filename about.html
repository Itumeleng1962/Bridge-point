About BridgePoint Advisory

Strategic advisors committed to inclusive growth.

BridgePoint Advisory is a fast-growing business advisory and consulting firm headquartered in Johannesburg, established to bridge opportunities between businesses, investors and markets with agile, client-centred advisory.

Our Story

We deliver strategic, financial and operational solutions that enable organisations to adapt, compete and thrive in an evolving business landscape, building a reputation for agility, innovation and client-centred advisory across Africa.

Mission

To bridge the gap between challenges and opportunities by delivering innovative advisory services that accelerate business growth and transformation.

Vision

To become a leading start-up advisory firm that supports entrepreneurs and businesses in achieving sustainable growth and competitiveness in Africa.

What Sets Us Apart

Projects are grounded in user research, performance data and brand clarity, ensuring strategies are practical, scalable and ready for execution.

Transformation

Transformation isn’t a pillar. It’s our centre of gravity.

BridgePoint Advisory is 100% Black owned with 70% Black women ownership. We intentionally deploy our expertise to close gaps in capital access, operational excellence and leadership representation.

  • Deep experience across financial services, development partners and multinational corporates.
  • Partners in ecosystems such as accelerators, incubators and impact investors.
  • Programmes tailored to township and rural enterprises as well as high-growth tech ventures.

Purpose

Empower inclusive growth by pairing strategy with the discipline to deliver. We believe in entrepreneurship as a lever for social and economic transformation.
